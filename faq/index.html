FAQ

Common Questions

What does Elemental Spirits mean

Pretty much it’s an NFT game where you can make some rewards — that’s Elemental Spirits in a nutshell — you get to collect battle and grow these cool magical spirits tied to stuff like fire water and all that — players can grab sell or beef up their spirits then throw them into fights with others or take on game challenges to score some goodies

How do I jump in

Easy — sign up on our site — snag your first elemental spirit from the Marketplace either by buying or earning it — then start battling doing quests or leveling up your crew to make them stronger

Do I need to buy NFTs to play

Nah you don’t have to — we hook you up with free spirits when you start — but if you wanna get a leg up you can grab some premium ones from the Marketplace

Gameplay Questions

What are the three big game modes

PvP Battles — go head-to-head with other players using spirits and their unique tricks
PvE Quests — roam the map tackle tasks and pick up useful loot
Spirit Evolution — mix spirits together and power them up to make them tougher

How do I level up my spirits

Just fuse spirits that are on the same level and toss in some game cash — each time you do they get stronger and rarer too

Can I sell my spirits

Yep every spirit’s up for grabs — sell buy or trade them as NFTs on our Marketplace

Tokens & Economies

What’s the ESPIRIT token

$ESPIRIT is the main in-game token that powers the Elemental Spirits economy — you earn it by winning battles, completing quests, or trading on the Marketplace. You can use $ESPIRIT to fuse and evolve your spirits, unlock premium content, or buy rare items. It’s also a tradable token, so if you’re good at the game, you can turn your skills into real value.
